Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

CSL Calculations is a WebView-based mobile application developed by VTsoft.

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users directly. It simply displays content from a website using a WebView.

However, the website shown inside the app may use third-party tools such as Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. These analytics services may collect information such as browser type, IP address, time spent on pages, and other usage patterns.

VTsoft does not access or control any data collected by the embedded website. For more information on how such data is used, please refer to the privacy policy of the website you are accessing through the app.
